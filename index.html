Definitions & Label Guide
Growing: sustained upward movement across recent valid weeks.
Emerging Growth: upward direction, but not yet stable.
Recovered: return near baseline after a sharp dip; not counted as growth.
Flat: no meaningful up/down change.
Declining: sustained downward movement.
Volatile: large swings without clear direction.
Reporting %: share of expected reports actually submitted.
Missing Reports: expected reports with no submitted attendance value.
Needs Attention: low reporting, missing reports, or zero-attendance risk.
